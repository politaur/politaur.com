Solaris EP is out now!

When I was reading Solaris by Stanislaw Lem I knew almost instantly: this is it! That's the inspiration I was looking for for my first EP. That book resonates with me so many levels, that I knew: I have to let free those feelings which that book created inside me.

So, this is it! My first EP.
